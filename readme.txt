Easy way to follow the EU Cookie directive, especially for Spain (more restrictive law).

== Description ==
Based on the JavaScript version by [Emilio Cobos](https://github.com/ecoal95/CookieTool/) .
This plugin shows a notice to your users warning them about the use of cookies in your site. It also shows a link to your (mandatory) privacy/cookie policy page.

== Installation ==
1. Upload \"wp-cookietool\" to the \"/wp-content/plugins/\" directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
1. Configure your settings.
1. Create a privacy/cookie policy page and place the [cookie-div] shortcode wherever you please.
